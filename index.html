A little intro

I’m a Microsoft.NET C# developer with over twelve years experience in architecting and developing software systems using industry recognized patterns and practises. I’m a proponent of test driven development (TDD) and the natural fit that it has with Agile and XP development methodologies. I’m interested in messaging based event driven architecture (EDA) and the loose coupling it achieves which in turn allows for scalable systems.

I’m also a proponent of domain driven design (DDD) as a way of bringing development closer to the business and effectively delivering business requirements whilst also delivering a system which mirrors in the code the way the business should run. Another paradigm I’m investigating and working with is command query responsibility segregation (CQRS) which nicely deals with the impedance mismatch between relational data structures and the object orientated manner in which we should be working with our subject matter as developers amongst many other pertinent advantages.

Most of all I believe in continuous learning. I develop software because I enjoy the constantly changing nature of the environment and that it is always in a state of flux, for the better.

As you can see I’m still learning… This is what it’s all about.
